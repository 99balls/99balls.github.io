    What are Puzzle games?

    Puzzle games are consist of different scenarios like, word puzzle game, crossword puzzle, picture puzzle, mahjong puzzle. These games will improve your intelligent and hand skills. In some of the puzzle games, you match the cards, in some of other puzzle games, you try to find the correct option or the word on the board.

    Online Puzzle games

    There are also some online puzzle games here too. You connect a platform and you play the game against players around of the world. These kind of games will be quiz based games. Try to be the winner in these kind of games by scoring most.
